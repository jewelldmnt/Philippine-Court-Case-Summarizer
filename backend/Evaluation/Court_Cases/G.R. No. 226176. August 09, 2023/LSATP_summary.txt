FACTS:
This is a Petition for Review on Certiorari (Petition) filed under Rule 45 of the Rules of Court by petitioners National Commission on Indigenous Peoples (NCIP), Zenaida Brigida Hamada-Pawid, Dionesia O. Banua, Conchita C. Calzado, Percy Brawner, Cosme Lambayon, Santos Unsad, and Basilio Wandag (collectively, petitioners) against respondent Macroasia Corporation (Macroasia), seeking to reverse and set aside the Amended Decision dated March 14, 2016 and the Resolution dated August 9, 2016, and to reinstate the Decision dated April 22, 2015, all promulgated by the Court of Appeals (CA) in the case docketed as CA-G.R. SP No. 124632. Macroasia filed a Joint Motion to Render Judgment Based on Compromise Agreement dated February 21, 2023, manifesting that the parties have finally resolved their differences and agreed to amicably settle the subject case, and submitting the Compromise Agreement dated February 1, 2023. Accordingly, Macroasia prayed that the case be declared closed and terminated based on the said Compromise Agreement.
